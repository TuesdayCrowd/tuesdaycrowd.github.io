Two tracks under one roof. A seasonal outdoor circuit. Coming to Naperville, Illinois — the west suburbs' first dedicated electric karting venue.

What we're building

Two indoor electric karting tracks — an intermediate circuit and a multi-level pro track with elevation changes — plus a seasonal outdoor circuit operating mid-April through mid-October during daylight hours.

Concessions, beer and wine, an arcade, and private rooms for birthdays, corporate events, and league racing. Powered by Ferkart electric karts with swappable batteries: quiet enough to operate indoors, fast enough to feel like the real thing.
